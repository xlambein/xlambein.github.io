Inspired by [Malwine](http://malweene.com/) and [Éléonore Mayola](http://www.elle-est-au-nord.com/), I am starting a series of mini zines on STEM subjects.  Today, I just finished the polished version of the first "Sci-Zine" on the _transit method_, a technique to find exoplanets.

[Click here to download the PDF and print it for yourself](/assets/pdf/scizine001-exoplanets-transit.pdf), or read it below.  Instructions on how to fold the zine (and also, how to make your own) [can be found here](http://www.rookiemag.com/2012/05/how-to-make-a-zine/).

Feel free to share this as you please <3
