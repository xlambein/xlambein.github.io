Sweet Potato Waffles

Serves 2.

Ingredients

Waffles

  • 1 sweet potato (~200g), grated
  • 200g flour
  • 15g sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3g dry yeast
  • 50g oil
  • 30ml water

Pumpkin Sauce

  • 4 tbsp pumpkin puree
  • 2 tsp tahini
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch cumin

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine the ingredients for the waffles then knead for 20 minutes by hand or with a mixer. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.

  2. Pre-heat the waffle iron. Form balls with the dough and cook each one for 2–4 minutes, until browned and crispy.

  3. Combine the sauce ingredients, and serve with the waffles.
